About me

Software Developer

Hey there! I am a candidate for the Bachelors of Data Science at the University of Waterloo, currently in my second year. I am a passionate programmer who tries to get the most out of every opportunity that presents itself and give my best in everything I do in life. I had dedicated myself to software development since my junior year in high school and had participated in many hackathons/start-ups where I had the chance to apply my skills and build a large network within the field of computer science. In a similar manner, through self-education I was able to pick up the necessary skills to built websites, construct complex algorithms, mine & present data using statistical analysis, and actively model automated systems for digital marketing. I am committed to lifelong learning regardless of where I am, how old I happen to be, or how many difficulties I may face along the way. Over the years I learned to center my focus around self-development, this helped me find the activities that truly bring happiness into my life, and challenge me to become better day after day.

Between September 2019 and March 2021 I’ve taken part in numerous extracurricular activities that have shaped me into who I am today. For starters, one of my proudest projects is FloorPlanner. Essentially, FloorPlanner is a business that I’ve put together which focuses on transforming blueprints of homes into 2D images, 3D images, and live 3D walkthroughs. The images showcase the layout of a home, the exact furniture, and give the viewers a realistic glimpse of the home as if they are walking through it in real life. The key focus of this business is to aid people who are looking to promote a property (e.g. rental Airbnb) by posting realistic images that encompass every aspect of a home (bird's eye view is one of the available features). I’ve constructed a website for this company, performed SEO/SEM, and acquired the necessary licensing to legally provide the offered services. I engineer custom floor plans using blueprints and deliver them to my clients at the best prices possible, I also happen to be the only provider in town. Every business project has taught me something new and I am always eager to learn, adapt, and attain new experiences wherever I go.
